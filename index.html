Welcome to
Trail Blazers

We are a non-profit organization that is aimed at helping women join the work place. We are for women of varying age ranges, trying to join the job market, both formal and informal sectors of the job spectrum.

What we do

We train for

Business Etiquette, Interview skills, Dress for Success, On boarding, Mock interviews and CV sculpting.

We mentor for

Work life balance, Breaking the glass ceiling ,Motivation 

Target Audiance

Women for 18 upward looking to start a carrer job. 

Vision

To edducate and train young women who are tying to get in the job market either in the formal or informal sector how to dress properply for an interview and how to create an adequate C.V for the right job.

GET INVOLVED

Your can support us in these 3 simple ways

Volunteer & Support

You are welcome to take a young lady under your sleeve and mentor her to be ready for the job market.

Donate

We are accept your generous donations of official wear to help a young lady ace her interview.

Partner with us

Corporate organizations are welcome to help this generous course.
